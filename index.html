Graduate Student in Vehicle Engineering

I am a graduate student focusing on intelligent driving, end-to-end autonomous parking, trajectory prediction, and closed-loop autonomous driving evaluation.

My research centers on building robust parking trajectory prediction systems by combining BEV representation learning, planner-prior trajectory refinement, and CARLA-based closed-loop validation.

Learning-based parking systems from perception to trajectory output.

📍 Trajectory Prediction and Planning

Predicting feasible, smooth, and executable parking trajectories.

🧭 BEV Representation Learning

Building bird's-eye-view scene representations for driving tasks.

🛠️ Planner-prior Residual Modeling

Refining traditional planning priors with learning-based residual correction.

🌍 CARLA Closed-loop Evaluation

Validating autonomous parking policies in simulation environments.

🤖 Multimodal Learning for AD

Exploring vision, language, and trajectory modeling for autonomous driving.

🎯 Reinforcement Learning

Studying long-horizon decision-making for closed-loop driving tasks.
